About Art Advisor

Art Advisor is a free-to-use global platform for finding, sharing and recommending art happenings around you. Our dream is to make art an accessible part of everyday life experiences and discourses all around the world. We promote everything from grassroot artists’ studios to highly established art institutions. We don’t curate it, you do!

We respect the art content and our user’s privacy. Art Advisor does not serve advertisements and does not sell information about its users to third parties. We don’t claim ownership over any Content that you post on the Art Advisor Services. Mutually we expect that you as a user have the rights and permission to the content that you post in our services. Also, only input valid art events and artist info. Other than fine art related events will be removed from Art Advisor. Please read more in our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Art Advisor is a service provided by Finnish non-profit artist-run company called Kunstventures Oy. The development and initial launch of Art Advisor has been kindly supported by Finnish cultural funds, namely Konstsamfundet, AVEK and Koneen säätiö. We are deeply grateful for the support our initiative has been blessed with now and in future. Art Advisor is a non-profit service, and is free for you to use in all it’s basic functions, but for the maintenance and upkeep, we will need to generate donation and grant based funding. If you feel the need and have the possibility to patron our cause, please contact support@artadvisor.fi.

Art Advisor Rules

created 19.10.2015

We of course believe that these are totally obvious to you as a respectful, thoughtful and loving person, but we urge everybody to keep in mind that:

Don’t post any personal information of any other users or third parties unless you have express permission from them to do so. Don’t use Art Advisor to intrude on other people’s privacy.

Don’t spam. Only input valid art events and artist info. Other than fine art related events will be removed from Art Advisor.

Don’t automate. Art Advisor is for human beings. It is against the rules to use a computer to register a lot of users on Art Advisor.

Don’t post content that belongs to third parties without their consent. Please make sure you have the right to post the content that you put on Art Advisor. In other words, only post content that you own or have permission to post.

Only post content that is legal, meaning you can’t post content that violates a law, rule or regulation.

Don’t register a username that you are not going to use yourself. Don’t sell your username. Don’t register a username just to prevent someone else from using it. Usernames that have not been actively used for an extended period will be reclaimed.

Don’t impersonate others. Please don’t pretend to be someone else.

Don’t try to circumvent or compromise our security or access non-public areas of the Art Advisor website. Don’t use robots or other automated technology to access Art Advisor. Do not scrape content from Art Advisor. Don’t overload our servers with traffic. Don’t upload or distribute viruses or malware to or through Art Advisor.

Art Advisor’s rules expressly forbid posting abusive content of any kind, including content of any kind that portrays or encourages violence towards people or animals. Hate speech, including denigrating comments involving race, sexual orientation, religious or political belief, age, whether they be personal or general, are not allowed.

If you break the rules we may contact you and ask you to fix the problem immediately.

If you don’t, your account may be suspended or deleted, your username forfeited, and your IP address may be permanently banned from logging into Art Advisor. We reserve the right to enforce or not to enforce these rules in whatever way we see fit, at our sole discretion.

Art Advisor’s rules and policies do not create a duty or contractual obligation for us to act in any particular manner. Art Advisor reserves the right to change these rules at any time.

If you notice abusive behavior, please report it immediately by emailing report@artadvisor.fi and we will look into it, and take necessary action as soon as possible. This email box is strictly for reporting abuse, and we will not respond to other requests sent here.
